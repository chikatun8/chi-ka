Music

CHI-KA is a Japanese singer. Born and raised in Okinawa. Because CHI-KA has always been surrounded by a beautiful nature and good music, she now has very beautiful and unforgettable voice.

In 2009, her song "Shikisai" was used in the 5th anniversary commercial for the Beach Tower Hotel Okinawa. CHI-KA collaborated with Ryota Nozaki (Jazztronik) to sing "Ponyo on the Cliff by the Sea" from the cover album "Ghibli meets Bossa Nova."

Her debut album, "With You" was released on April 7th, 2010 and produced by JAZZIDA GRANDE. She always express her motto, "always be positive," to girls who work in urban cities.

She sings the opening song of "Rune Factory Oceans," or "Tides of Destiny" as it's known internationally (PS3/Wii role-playing game developed by Neverland Co.),which was released on February 24th, 2011.

On February 26th, 2011, her album "With You Rune Factory Oceans Deluxe Edition" was released.

On May 11th, 2011, citron, formed by the guitarist Daisuke "DSK" Kojima from Port of Notes, CHI-KA and Kiyoshi Ikegami fromJAZZIDA GRANDE, released the cover album "Utael BOSSA." This album may be very familiar to Japanese people because it is sung by young Japanese students.

From May to October, 2013, her song was featured in a TV commercial for TOYOTA Okinawa.

On December 3rd, 2014, three songs from her single album 「Wings of Heart」(featured in TOYOTA Okinawa's TV commercial) went on sale in Japan.
